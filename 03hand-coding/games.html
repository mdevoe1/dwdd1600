List of all Resident Evil Games released sinced 1996

All main games for you to know

Resident Evil (1996) / Remake (2002 / 2022 HD Remaster)
The game that started it all. S.T.A.R.S. members Chris Redfield and Jill Valentine investigate strange murders in the Arklay Mountains, discovering the zombie-infested Spencer Mansion and uncovering the Umbrella Corporation’s bioweapons research.
Resident Evil 2 (1998) / Remake (2019)
Leon S. Kennedy, a rookie cop, and Claire Redfield, searching for her brother, arrive in Raccoon City during a zombie outbreak caused by the G-Virus. They must survive horrors inside the Raccoon City Police Department and beyond.
Resident Evil 3: Nemesis (1999) / Remake (2020)
Set around the same time as RE2, Jill Valentine tries to escape Raccoon City while being relentlessly pursued by Nemesis, a bioweapon designed to hunt surviving S.T.A.R.S. members.
Resident Evil CODE: Veronica (2000)
Claire Redfield continues her search for Chris and ends up imprisoned on Rockfort Island. Chris comes to her rescue as the siblings face new B.O.W.s and the insane remnants of the Ashford family.
Resident Evil 4 (2005) / Remake (2023)
Leon S. Kennedy is sent to a rural Spanish village to rescue the U.S. President’s daughter, Ashley Graham, from a cult infected with the mind-controlling Las Plagas parasite.
Resident Evil 5 (2009)
Chris Redfield and new partner Sheva Alomar investigate bio-organic weapon smuggling in Africa, confronting the return of Albert Wesker and uncovering the origin of the Progenitor Virus.
Resident Evil 6 (2012)
Four interwoven campaigns starring Leon, Chris, Ada Wong, and newcomer Jake Muller (Wesker’s son) tackle global bio-terror attacks and a new virus called the C-Virus.
Resident Evil 7: Biohazard (2017)
The series shifts to first-person horror. Ethan Winters searches for his missing wife, Mia, in a derelict Louisiana plantation controlled by the terrifying, infected Baker family.
Resident Evil Village (2021)
Continuing Ethan’s story, he searches for his kidnapped daughter, Rose, in a mysterious Eastern European village ruled by monstrous lords, including the iconic Lady Dimitrescu.
Resident Evil 4 Remake (2023)
A faithful yet modernized reimagining of RE4, with refined gameplay, deeper character moments, and expanded story elements while staying true to the original plot.
Resident Evil 9: Requiem (2026, upcoming)
Not yet released, but reportedly will conclude the Ethan Winters and Rose saga, focusing on new threats tied to the series’ long history of bioweapons.
